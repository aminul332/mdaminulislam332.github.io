Major Design Project #01

Stadium Renovation Design

Concept: “Coastal Identity & Functional Renewal”

Project Description

This project proposes the renovation of an existing stadium in Khulna through a context-driven and functional design approach. Based on intensive site analysis, key limitations were addressed through improved spatial planning and user experience.

The concept is inspired by coastal seashell geometry, reflected in the stadium’s form and overhead covering system. Container-style restaurant spaces reference the nearby Mongla Port, strengthening regional identity. The design emphasizes minimalism, durability, and climate-responsive material selection suitable for coastal conditions.

Key Features

  • Coastal-inspired form
  • Improved circulation
  • Integrated commercial spaces
  • Climate-responsive design

Materials Used

• Reinforced Concrete
• Corrosion-Resistant Steel
• Tensile Roofing
• Glass Panels & Local Materials
