Small Cottage Design

Space for Thyself Concept

Project Overview

This conceptual retreat, titled "Space for Thyself," focuses on creating a minimalist living environment that harmonizes with nature. As a civil engineering student, the challenge was to integrate aesthetic architectural form with structural efficiency.

The design utilizes sustainable materials and focuses on spatial optimization for small-scale living. Every square inch is calculated to provide maximum utility while maintaining a sense of openness and tranquility.

Technical Tools

  • Revit (Modeling)
  • Lumion (Rendering)
  • Photoshop (Editing)
