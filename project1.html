Project Case Study #01

Small Cottage Design

Conceptual Design: "Space for Thyself"

Project Overview

The "Space for Thyself" cottage is a modern architectural solution for high-density minimalist living. As a BECM student, the goal was to design a structure that is not only visually stunning but also structurally sound and cost-effective.

The design prioritizes natural light, ventilation, and a low carbon footprint, utilizing sustainable materials to ensure long-term durability and aesthetic harmony with the surroundings.

Materials Specifications

Reinforced Concrete (Grade 30)
Low-E Energy Efficient Glass
Sustainable Timber Decking
High-Performance Smart Bricks

Technical Stack

  • Autodesk Revit
  • Lumion 12
  • AutoCAD Drafting
  • MS Excel Analysis
