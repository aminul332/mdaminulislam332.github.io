Civil Engineering Case Study #01

Small Cottage Design

Conceptual Design: "Space for Thyself"

Project Description

The "Space for Thyself" cottage is a modern architectural solution for high-density minimalist living. As a BECM student, the goal was to design a structure that is not only visually stunning but also structurally sound and cost-effective. The design prioritizes natural light, ventilation, and a low carbon footprint.

Materials Used

• Reinforced Concrete: Grade 30 for main frame.
• Low-E Glass: Energy efficient window glazing.
• Timber Decking: Sustainable treated wood.
• Smart Bricks: For thermal insulation.

Software Used

  • Autodesk Revit
  • Lumion 12
  • AutoCAD (Drafting)
  • MS Excel (Costing)

Project Timeline
